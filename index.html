Service Policy

I focus on enhancing your natural beauty through professional color grading and skin texture refinement. Notice: To preserve your authentic self, I do not offer structural alterations (such as body slimming or facial reshaping).

I do not take references from other photographers' work. Every photographer has their own unique shooting and editing style. If you are looking for a style that is different from my portfolio, I would kindly recommend finding a photographer whose work more closely matches what you have in mind.

Travel

Base Location

North Melbourne (VIC 3051)

Travel Policy (One-way)

  • Included: No additional travel fee for locations within a 1-hour public transport commute from North Melbourne.
  • Travel Fee: A fee starting from $50 applies for locations exceeding 1 hour or with limited public transport access.
  • Locations that are difficult to reach by public transport may not be available for shooting. Please contact me in advance to check.
